Malia Dragon Boat Racing Application

Malia Dragon Boat Racing Team travels out of state many times a year. They approached my team with the problem of organizing accomodation when they travel for races. Technologies used: AngularJS, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Express.js, Node.js, Passport, PostgresSQL
